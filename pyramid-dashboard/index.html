Stops

  • Primary trailing stop: the symbol's own structure — last qualified swing low / support.
  • Trail only tightens, never loosens.
  • Stop logic attached to EVERY active leg immediately after entry. No unprotected legs.
  • Lime Weather is a tightening overlay / kill-switch only — it is not the trailing engine.

Bad leg handling

  • One leg hitting its 1R hard stop closes THAT leg only.
  • Proven legs ride under the same rules.
  • Per-leg tracking: entry price, qty, risk, active/closed state. Aggregate avgCost is not sufficient.
  • Trim is one-shot per leg, guarded by an active flag — not every bar.
